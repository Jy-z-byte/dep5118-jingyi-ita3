MRT Walk Stress Projects

MRT Walk Stress Projects explores the relationship between Singapore’s MRT network and population pressure.

The storymap analyzes population distribution and MRT accessibility using various datasets including population by planning area and MRT station. The analysis reveals how well high-density areas are served by MRT stations.

The crowdsourcing map allows users to contribute real-time insights by identifying MRT stations experiencing heavy foot traffic. This user-generated data can help refine our understanding of congestion patterns and accessibility issues.

By combining spatial analysis with public input, this project provides a comprehensive perspective on how Singapore’s MRT system supports its growing population, highlighting areas for potential improvement.
